How It Works

The ArtuPower Technology

Our patented H₂O-CH fuel delivery system integrates alkane gas and water vapour into the intake manifold, harmonising combustion and delivering measurable gains in efficiency, torque, and emissions.

Gas-Diesel Integration

Alkane gas (LPG, CNG, BioLPG, BioCNG) is mixed with water vapour and introduced into the diesel engine intake. The blend is calibrated by the ArtuPower ECU in real time.

Combustion Optimisation

Water vapour acts as a catalyst, preventing engine knock and stabilising compression temperatures. The result is increased piston pressure, improved torque, and up to 10% efficiency gain.

Safe Failsafe Design

If the ArtuPower system detects any malfunction, the vehicle automatically reverts to diesel-only operation. Full monitoring and control via a dedicated mobile app.
